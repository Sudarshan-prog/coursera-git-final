# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity, level of experience, nationality, or religion.

## Our Standards
We expect all contributors to:
- Be respectful
- Use inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism
- Help create a welcoming community

## Unacceptable Behavior
Includes:
- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Trolling
- Personal attacks

## Reporting
Report violations by opening an issue or emailing the maintainer.

## Enforcement
Community leaders may remove comments, commits, or contributors violating these rules.
